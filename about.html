We, the students of Wesea (Western SouthEast Asia), coming together from the diverse regions of Northeast India and Northern parts of West Bengal, Chittagong, Mymensingh, parts of Sylhet in Bangladesh, and Kachin, Chin, and Naga areas of Myanmar, united by our common heritage and aspirations, solemnly resolve to form the Wesean Student Federation, otherwise known as the WSF. This federation stands as a beacon of unity, integrity, and fraternity among all Wesean students. Guided by the undying spirit of solidarity, we dedicate ourselves to protecting indigenous rights, promoting our rich Wesean culture, and enhancing the educational and intellectual prowess of our student body.

Resolved to dismantle the barriers of ethnonationalism and internal discord, we pledge to uphold the values of unity and integrity, fostering an environment where every voice is heard and valued. We commit to standing against all forms of division and strife among Weseans, ensuring a cohesive and harmonious community.

Through this federation, we vow to champion the collective interests of Wesean students, safeguarding our traditions and rights while paving the way for a future replete with academic excellence and cultural prosperity. On this historic day, the 17th of April 2024, we adopt, enact, and give to ourselves this constitution, binding ourselves to these principles for the advancement and unity of all Weseans.

About Us

The Wesean Student Federation (WSF) is headquartered in Shillong, a symbolic site as the former capital of the Northeast Presidency under British administration. Our federation strives to empower students through advocacy, education, and leadership across the Wesea region.

Regions Served

  • Northeast India, including the Gorkha and Koch areas of West Bengal
  • Bangladesh: Chittagong, Sylhet, Mymensingh
  • Myanmar: Kachin-Chin and Naga areas
